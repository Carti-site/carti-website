Artist Bio

Playboi Carti, born Jordan Terrell Carter, is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter known for his experimental style, fashion influence, and enigmatic persona. He gained mainstream success with his 2017 debut and went on to release critically acclaimed projects like *Die Lit* and *Whole Lotta Red*.
